Where to Find Climbs

As for climbing spots, the most popular app is Mountain Project. It is the standard and it is free to use. It has an interactive map that will show you what is around you, and normally has pretty good directions on how to find each boulder. It also can be used for top rope, sport, alpine, and trad climbing.
